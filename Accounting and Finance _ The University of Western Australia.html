Define your career with Accounting and Finance

Define your career with an accredited degree through the Accounting and Finance department at the world-renowned UWA Business School. Our flexible and comprehensive courses will provide you with an accredited degree through leading industry bodies and an enhanced education pathway preparing our students for a career in the global business world.

UWA’s strong connections with industry leaders and global research collaborations provide students and graduates with a critical edge now and throughout their career. Our corporate partners influence our course structures and teaching practises to stay at the forefront of industry trends and developments. We examine changing practises within employer requirements from recent graduates to ensure these are incorporated into the student journey.

UWA is ranked in the world’s top 100 for Accounting and Finance in the 2018 QS World University Rankings. We are a leader in providing innovative education and a strong emphasis on developing research within the field, to not only stay ahead of industry development but also influence developments.

A degree in Accounting and Finance can provide with endless industry possibilities in a range of positions. Our graduates follow career paths in the private and public sector, public accounting firms, small business and self-employment. Professional accountants are employed as company directors, board members, chief executive officers and partners in business.

Welcome from the Head of Department

Professor Lee Smales | Head of Accounting and Finance

Welcome to the Department of Accounting and Finance at UWA Business School.  As Head of Department, I’m pleased to introduce you to a vibrant academic community that values curiosity, innovation, and impact. Here, you will find a curriculum designed to challenge and inspire, led by internationally renowned faculty committed to academic excellence and real-world impact. 

In today’s complex financial world, the ability to understand and analyze economic realities is more important than ever. Our programs are designed to equip you with the technical expertise, critical thinking skills, and ethical foundations essential for a successful career in accounting, finance, and beyond.

Whether you aspire to become a financial analyst, auditor, CFO, or investment banker, at UWA you’ll find a rigorous curriculum, dedicated faculty, and strong industry connections to support your journey. We look forward to welcoming you into our dynamic and supportive learning environment.

Study accounting and finance courses at UWA

Our internationally accredited postgraduate courses can boost employment opportunities around the world. These practical courses are designed for graduates already working in the finance or business sectors, as well as those with a non-finance background who wish to move into a career in finance or business.

  • Graduate Diploma in Commerce (Chartered Accounting)
  • Master of Commerce
  • Master of Applied Finance
  • Master of Professional Accounting
  • Doctor of Philosophy (Accounting and Finance)

The Accounting and Finance undergraduate majors offer a variety of career paths and a broad range of options, allowing you to combine subjects in a way that matches your career goals and personal interests.
